            <h1> MOLO IS CHILDREN'S FASHION THAT'S HEAVY ON THE CHARM</h1>
<p>Molo’s design feeds a child’s imagination with strong colours, fun prints, exciting shapes and bold combinations. At Molo, we are completely crazy about prints because we appreciate that they help give character to even the smallest personalities. </p>
<p>Our style is urban with funky, colourful designs that often have a humorous twist or are just quirky enough to get noticed- but never at the price of function and quality.
