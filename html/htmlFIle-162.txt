		<h1>Besam is a part of ASSA ABLOY Entrance Systems</h1>



	
	<p id="ctl00_bodyRegion_pageRegion_ContentRegion_MainContentRegion_PageContent_ctrlPreamble" class="preamble">Are you looking for Besam products? Then you have come to the right place. The Besam brand has been part of ASSA ABLOY Entrance Systems for a while, and you can be sure to find the automatic pedestrian entrances you are looking for, whether it is <a href="https://www.assaabloyentrance.com/en/aaes/aaes/products/automatic-doors/">sliding doors</a>, <a href="https://www.assaabloyentrance.com/en/aaes/aaes/products/automatic-doors/">swing doors</a> or <a href="https://www.assaabloyentrance.com/en/aaes/aaes/products/automatic-doors/">revolving doors</a></p>
	<div class="text-max-width">
		<p>Besam was established in 1962 with the development of automatic doors for the healthcare sector. In 1970 the business expanded with sliding door solutions and in 1985 included a portfolio of revolving doors. As the need for automatic doors grew in industries like transportation and retail, Besam expanded to several markets worldwide.</p>
<p>Besam was acquired by ASSA ABLOY in 2002 and is now a product brand of ASSA ABLOY Entrance Systems. For you this makes no difference, we have gathered the expertise and experience and still offer superior <a href="/en/aaes/aaes/products/automatic-doors/">Besam automatic pedestrian doors</a>. To complement your automatic doors, we also offer a broad range of&nbsp;<a href="/en/aaes/aaes/products/industrial-and-commercial-doors/">industrial-, commercial-</a>, <a href="/en/aaes/aaes/products/high-performance-doors/">high-performance-</a>&nbsp;and <a href="/en/aaes/aaes/products/hangar-doors/">hangar doors</a>, as well as <a href="/en/aaes/aaes/products/loading-dock-equipment/">loading dock equipment</a>&nbsp;and <a href="/en/aaes/aaes/maintenance-and-modernization/">service</a> from leading product brands Crawford, Albany and Megadoor.</p>
<p>Learn more and find your product category below.&nbsp;</p>
